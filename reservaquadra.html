Case Study

A centralized booking flow for sports courts.

ReservaQuadra was designed to reduce the fragmented process of finding and booking courts. Users can search by location, sport, date, and time; facility owners can manage availability, pricing, maintenance, and booking operations.

Product

Core Features

Real-time Booking

Availability search and reservation workflows for court, sport, date, and time combinations.

Map Discovery

Location-based search with Mapbox and geocoding support for multi-location establishments.

Payments

Stripe payment integration with transparent pricing and reservation checkout flows.

Facility Admin

Dashboards for courts, pricing, availability, maintenance scheduling, cancellations, and statistics.

Notifications

Automated WhatsApp and email updates through Twilio and SendGrid.

Deployment

Containerized backend on Render, frontend on Vercel, and PostgreSQL-backed persistence.
