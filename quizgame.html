Case Study

Real-time quiz gameplay with separate host and player experiences.

This project was built from scratch for creating, managing, hosting, and playing multiplayer quiz games. It demonstrates full-stack CRUD workflows, account management, game state, player onboarding, timed questions, scoreboards, and post-game statistics.

Flow

How It Works

01

The host logs in, creates or selects a quiz, configures questions, and starts a game lobby.

02

Players enter their information and game PIN, then wait in the player lobby until the host starts.

03

Questions are shown with a countdown while each player answers on their own device.

04

After each question, the system shows partial scores; after the game, it presents final rankings and statistics.
